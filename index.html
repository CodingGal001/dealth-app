Who we are

Our Little Story

We are a group of creative minds with a passion to provide meaningful
experiences for individuals concerning their wills and assets. We
believe death and illness are inevitable and can occur at any time.And
as such, people deserve the opportunity to make decisions regarding
their wills and property to reduce the stress and disputes among
deceased families.

How it works

Throughout your journey at LiveOn, we protect your data. We also keep track of you by sending notification if you remain dormant for a
time. Follow the step below to get started.

  • Register and create a profile

  • Verify your account

  • Upload your document

  • Upload your next of kin document

Register and create a profile

The first step can be overwhelming since it is the start of your journey,
but at LiveOn, we would love to walk withyou and get to know you.

Verify your account

For security reasons, we will need you to verify your account. These will
safeguard you and also give room for smooth usage of our application.

Upload your document

You are almost, with just a few more steps to take. Start by uploading
any confidential document you want to safeguard and also what we can
support you with.

Upload your next of kin details

At LiveOn, the power of attorney belongs to you, but by uploading your
next of kin details, we will archive it their details until when needed.
